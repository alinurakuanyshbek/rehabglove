How it works

Simple. Safe. Effective.

Three steps from setup to recovery.

01
🧤
Put on the glove
Wear the lightweight rehabilitation glove on the affected hand. Powered by a portable battery — no cables needed.
02
🎙
Speak a command
Say "Open" or "Close" — the AI voice recognition works offline. Choose therapy mode: 5, 10, or 15 minutes.
03
📊
Track progress
Session data syncs to the dashboard automatically. Doctor monitors compliance and adjusts the therapy plan remotely.
Features

Built for real rehabilitation

Every feature designed with stroke patients and rehabilitation specialists in mind.

🎙
Offline Voice Control
Vosk AI recognizes commands without internet. Works in rural areas and hospitals with poor connectivity.
📱
Doctor Dashboard
Real-time monitoring of all patients. Compliance tracking, session history, and therapy plan management.
🔋
Fully Portable
12V portable battery powers the device for hours. Use at home, in transport, or outdoors.
🛡
Safety First
Emergency stop button and automatic stop on resistance detection keep patients safe.
☁️
Auto Sync
When Wi-Fi is available, all offline sessions sync automatically to the cloud dashboard.
🎯
Therapy Modes
Three automatic modes (5, 10, 15 min) plus manual control. Doctor sets daily goals for each patient.
